Pockets Recipe

Description

This dish is a fan favorite. Great for a fast paced household where sometime, you just need to eat on the go. Pockets can be made savory or sweet. Come back next week for a way to make them into a delicious pastry.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour(480 grams)
  • 1/2 cup sour cream(113 grams)
  • 1/4 cup softened butter (57 grams)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • tsp salt
  • 2 1/2 tsp active yeast (1 packet)
    •

    Filling

  • 1tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 1/2 tbsp chili powder
  • 250 grams of ground beef
  • 170 grams cubed cheddar cheese
  • 1 large egg mixed with 1 tbsp water to spread on dough
    •

    Steps

    1. Put all the dough ingredients into a bowl and mix.
    2. Put all the filling ingredients into a pan and cook them.
    3. Put the fillin in the dough
    4. Put the dough into the oven.
    5. EAT!
